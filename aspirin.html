Act I — The Problem

Relief… and pain

Salicylic acid eases pain, but its phenolic –OH irritates the stomach.

We’ll fix it in Act II.

Act II — The Breakthrough

Hoffmann’s choice (1897)

Mask the phenolic –OH by acetylation → acetylsalicylic acid (aspirin).

  • Masks acidity → tolerability ↑
  • Acts as a prodrug → hydrolyzes in vivo
  • Enables COX acetylation → analgesia, antiplatelet
